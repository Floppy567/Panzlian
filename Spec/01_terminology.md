# 1. Terminology

This specification uses the following terms consistently:

- **Nucleos**: the identity core of a word group
- **Orbital**: a modifier bound to a nucleos
- **Strong link**: defining orbital with internal authority
- **Weak link**: autonomous modifier affecting interpretation
- **Word group**: a 2D semantic unit composed of one nucleos and orbitals
- **Relational bridge ('Y)**: an external operator expressing relativity between groups

Terms defined here are considered reserved.
